Chopsticks

You might cross your chopsticks and leave them on the bowl to rest your hands sometimes during the meal. However, this action is considered bad manners unless you are finished eating. It's preferable to use a chopsticks rest instead. But if you don't have one, you can make one by the paper wrapper.

How to make chopsticks rest from its paper wrapper

Step 1

Fold the wrapper in half.

Step 2

Fold it in half again. Now it's a quarter of the original length.

Step 3

Fold it lengthwise in half.

It should look like this.

Step 4

Push in the triangular area of both sides.

It should look like this.

Step 5

Push down the top of the mountain to make it flat.

It should look like this.

All done

Place chopsticks on chopsticks rest.
